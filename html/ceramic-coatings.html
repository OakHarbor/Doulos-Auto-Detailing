Ceramic Coatings For Cars

Sometimes a typical wax isn't enough to protect your cars paint from the hazards of nature berating your cars exterior. What makes ceramic coatings unique is that they are either a liquid polymer or quartz compound that bonds with your cars exterior paint and creates a longer lasting shine with better protection.

At Doulos Auto Detailing, we use only the best products for your vehicle that we would trust on our own. We have two preferred ceramic coating products, GTechniq ceramic coatings and Cquartz UK 3.0.

7 Benefits of Ceramic Coatings

  • Protection against UV rays: Have you ever noticed how the sun fades the colors on posters? The same thing happens to the paint color on your car. UV rays can even crack the paint, exposing the metal underneath and allowing it to oxidize and rust. Ceramic coatings adds a layer of protection that is chemically bonded to your paint that prevents the UV rays from penetrating and damaging the paint.

  • Prevent chemical etching and stains: There are naturally occurring acidic contaminants in the air that can come in contact with your cars paint and chemically bond with it if it is left to sit on your car for too long. This is called chemical etching. These contaminants are etched into the surface of the paint. Ceramic coatings prevent these minerals and contaminants from bonding with the paint, keeping it smooth and protected.

  • Cost Effective: Typical waxing can last about 3 months on average until you need to get another waxing to keep your paint protected. But with ceramic coatings, they can last a few years before needing to be replaced and is much more durable than the wax treatments. So rather than spending money on getting multiple waxes a year, it's more economic to get a ceramic coating once every few years and have a stronger protective shield against the elements.

  • Enhanced Gloss Finish: If your car's finish is lacking luster, a ceramic coating can enhance the reflective properties of your vehicles paint giving it that showroom shine you see on luxury cars or new cars. It protects your cars paint while looking good doing it!

  • Repels water and prevents spotting: Water droplets that fall from the sky collect all kinds of debris, dirt, minerals, and grime on the way down to Earth. When they land on your car and eventually evaporate, all of that sky grime is left behind to sit on your car, which creates water spots. The right products will have hydrophobic properties that make water droplets bead instead of pooling on your vehicles surface, reducing water spots and mineral build up.

  • Makes washing easier: Since the ceramic coating repels water and other contaminants, you won't have to scrub as hard to remove them from your vehicles surface. They will just slide right off with little wahsing effort and make cleaning your car's exterior a breeze.

  • No more waxing!: With ceramic coatings, there's no more need to wax or buff your vehicle every few months since a ceramic coating does everything a wax coating does while lasting longer and enduring more stress. Looking For Ceramic Coating in Los Angeles?

Ceramic coatings are definitely a fantastic way to preserve and maintain not only the look and shine of your car, but also its resell value should the time come to do so. But while they seem like the one cure all for protection from scratches and other potential harms to your vehicle's exterior, there are no substitute for more advanced protective applications such as Paint Protection Film (PPE). For the best protection, we recommend a mix by using PPE on the most at risk and vulnerable parts of your car and then adding ceramic coatings to the rest of the paint on the body of your vehicle.
