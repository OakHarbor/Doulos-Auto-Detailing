Paint Protection

Doulos Auto Detailing employs a number of paint protection measures including Paint Protection Film, full body waxing, polishing, ceramic coatings, scratch protection, and washes. We take our job seriously when it comes to the look and durability of your car's paint job and we can make the right recommendations for paint protection measures based on you cars unique situation and your budget.

Polishing Services

Polishing your car's exterior has many benefits, including removing any scratches or defects, paint correction, reversing paint fading, add a glossy shine, enhancing the color depth, rejuvination. At Doulos, we know the exact amount of pressure to use, understand the differences between new and older paint, and we can look at your car and know the type of polishing compound to use, the pest pad to use, and have the best tools for the job.

Waxing Services

A car wax is among the most common and basic of all paint protection services because of how cost affective it is. If you want to keep your car looking its best and retaining its value, a regular waxing and wash is the most basic of services needed to accomplish those goals. It's recommended to get your vehicle waxed and washed every 3 months or so to keep the wax layer from wearing away completely and restoring it's protective qualities.

Complete Interior Detailing

We steam clean EVERYTHING, carpets, seats (upholstry and leather seats, cleaning the dashboard and center console, door panels, and even the cup holders. Every crevice of your car's interior will eb thoroughly cleaned down to the smallest of details.

Clay Bar Treatment

A clay bar treatment uses actual "detailing" clay bars specifically designed for using on cars to remove contaminants from the paint's surface like rail dust, brake dust, or other industrial contaminants. This method is non-abrasive and safe to use as it does not remove the clear coat when being used.

Wheel Cleaning

Wheel care is important to stay on top of to keep your car looking its best. When it comes to protecting your wheels, we remove all contaminants, polish them to shine lustering shine, and add protection from brake dust and natural elements. It is recommended to have a thorough wheel cleaning with layer protection at least every 2 to 3 months.

Window Cleaning

Part of our full interior detail, we remove any and all streaks, water spots, and dust from the interior and exterior sides of all your car's windows leaving you with a perfectly clear glass that looks like it isn't even there. You can drive confidently with a clear and unobstructed view of the road and enjoy the streak-free views.
